## Get started in 5 minutes

This guide will walk you through creating your Tykode account, setting up your profile, exploring our services, and submitting your first service request.

<Note>
  **No credit card required** - Creating an account and requesting quotes is completely free.
</Note>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    Navigate to the Tykode platform and sign up with your email and password.

    The authentication system uses Better-Auth with email/password authentication. You'll need:
    - A valid email address
    - A secure password (minimum 8 characters)

    <CodeGroup>
    ```bash Sign up endpoint
    POST /api/auth/signup
    Content-Type: application/json

    {
      "email": "jean@entreprise.com",
      "password": "SecurePass123",
      "name": "Jean Dupont"
    }
    ```
    </CodeGroup>

    <Note>
      Your account information is stored securely in our PostgreSQL database managed by Supabase, with passwords encrypted using industry-standard hashing.
    </Note>

    After submitting the form:
    1. You'll receive a confirmation that your account has been created
    2. You'll be automatically signed in
    3. Your session will be maintained securely with HTTP-only cookies

    <Warning>
      Make sure to verify your email address to ensure you receive important notifications about your projects.
    </Warning>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set up your profile">
    Complete your profile to help us serve you better.

    Navigate to your dashboard and add:
    - **Personal information**: First name, last name
    - **Contact details**: Phone number (optional)
    - **Company information**: Company name (if applicable)
    - **Profile picture**: Upload an avatar (optional)

    This information will be pre-filled in your service requests, saving you time.

    <Tip>
      Adding your phone number allows our team to reach you faster for urgent project updates.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Explore our services">
    Browse through our 9 professional digital services to find the right solution for your business.

    Visit the Services page to discover:

      </Card>

        Logo and branding in 1-2 weeks from €1,200
      </Card>

        SEO and advertising from €600/month

    </CardGroup>

    Each service page provides:
    - Detailed description of what's included
    - Estimated delivery timeline
    - Starting price range
    - Example use cases
  </Step>

  <Step title="Submit a service request">
    Ready to start your project? Use our multi-step contact form to get a personalized quote.

    The contact form includes three steps:

    ### Step 1: Your information
    Provide your contact details:
    - First name and last name (required)
    - Email address (required)
    - Phone number (optional)
    - Company name (optional)

    ### Step 2: Your project
    Describe what you need:
    - **Project type**: Select from 8 categories (website, e-commerce, mobile app, business software, visual identity, marketing, AI/automation, or other)
    - **Budget**: Optional budget range to help us tailor our proposal
    - **Timeline**: When you need the project completed

    <CodeGroup>
    ```typescript Project types
    const projectTypes = [
      { value: "site-vitrine", label: "Site vitrine" },

      { value: "identite-visuelle", label: "Identité visuelle" },
      { value: "marketing", label: "Marketing digital" },
      { value: "ia-automatisation", label: "IA & Automatisation" },
      { value: "autre", label: "Autre projet" }
    ]
    ```

    ```typescript Budget ranges

      { value: "moins-1k", label: "Moins de 1 000 €" },
      { value: "1k-3k", label: "1 000 € – 3 000 €" },

      { value: "a-definir", label: "À définir ensemble" }
    ]
    ```
    </CodeGroup>

    ### Step 3: Project details
    Provide a detailed description:
    - **Message**: Describe your project context, objectives, desired features, target audience, and any constraints (minimum 20 characters)
    - **How did you hear about us**: Optional feedback on how you found Tykode
    - **Privacy policy**: Accept our data processing terms (required)

    The form includes a summary of all your information before submission.

    <CodeGroup>
    ```bash Contact API endpoint
    POST /api/contact
    Content-Type: application/json

    {
      "firstName": "Jean",
      "lastName": "Dupont",
      "email": "jean@entreprise.com",
      "phone": "+594 694 45 86 55",
      "company": "Mon Entreprise",
      "projectType": "e-commerce",
      "budget": "3k-7k",
      "timeline": "2-4mois",
      "message": "Je souhaite créer une boutique en ligne pour vendre...",
      "howDidYouHear": "google",
      "acceptPrivacy": true
    }
    ```
    </CodeGroup>

    <Note>
      The form includes **anti-spam protection** with honeypot fields and **rate limiting** (maximum 3 submissions per hour per email address).
    </Note>

    After submission:
    1. Your request is saved in our Supabase database
    2. You receive an immediate confirmation on screen
    3. Our team is notified and will review your request
    4. We'll contact you within 24 hours with a personalized response

    <Warning>
      Make sure your email address is correct - this is how we'll send you the quote and project details.
    </Warning>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What happens next?

After submitting your service request:

<Steps>
  <Step title="24-hour response">
    Our team reviews your request and prepares a personalized proposal. We'll contact you within 24 business hours via email or phone.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Free consultation">
    We schedule a free consultation call to:
    - Understand your specific needs
    - Discuss technical requirements
    - Clarify project scope and timeline
    - Answer any questions you have
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Detailed quote">
    You'll receive a comprehensive quote including:
    - Exact pricing breakdown
    - Detailed project timeline
    - List of deliverables
    - Payment terms
    - Support and maintenance options
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Project kickoff">
    Once you approve the quote:
    - We assign a dedicated project team
    - Set up regular check-in meetings
    - Begin development with full transparency
    - Keep you updated at every milestone
  </Step>
</Steps>
