Your profile is where you manage your personal information, account settings, and preferences on the Tykode platform.

## Accessing Your Profile

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open user menu">
    Click on your avatar or username in the top-right corner of the navigation header.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Profile">
    Choose "Profile" or "Account Settings" from the dropdown menu.
  </Step>
  <Step title="View and edit">
    Your profile page will display all your account information and settings.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Profile Sections

### Personal Information

Manage your basic account details:

**Name and Contact**
- Full name
- Email address (used for login and notifications)
- Phone number (optional)
- Company or organization name

<Note>
  Email changes require verification through a confirmation link sent to your new email address.
</Note>

**Profile Picture**
- Upload a custom avatar image
- Supported formats: JPG, PNG, WebP
- Recommended size: 400x400 pixels
- Maximum file size: 2MB

### Account Settings

**Authentication**
- Change your password
- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA)
- Manage active sessions
- View login history

<Info>
  We recommend enabling two-factor authentication for enhanced account security.
</Info>

**Email Preferences**
- Marketing communications
- Product updates
- Security alerts (cannot be disabled)
- Weekly digest summaries

### Privacy Settings

Control your data and privacy:

- Profile visibility settings
- Data sharing preferences
- Cookie consent management
- Download your data (GDPR compliance)

## Updating Your Profile

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to profile settings">
    Access your profile through the user menu in the header.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Edit information">
    Click the "Edit" button or directly modify the fields you want to update.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save changes">
    Click "Save Changes" at the bottom of the form. You'll see a confirmation message when the update is successful.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Form Validation

The profile form includes real-time validation:

- **Email**: Must be a valid email format
- **Name**: Minimum 2 characters
- **Phone**: Optional, but must be valid if provided
- **Password**: Minimum 8 characters with mix of letters and numbers

<Note>
  Required fields are marked with an asterisk (*). You must complete these before saving.
</Note>

## Password Management

### Changing Your Password

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to security settings">
    Navigate to Profile → Security or click "Change Password" in your profile.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter current password">
    Verify your identity by entering your current password.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set new password">
    Enter your new password twice to confirm. The password must meet security requirements.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm change">
    Click "Update Password". You'll receive an email confirmation of the password change.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Password Requirements

- Minimum 8 characters
- At least one uppercase letter
- At least one number
- At least one special character (recommended)

### Forgot Password

If you've forgotten your password:

1. Click "Forgot Password" on the login page
2. Enter your email address
3. Check your email for a password reset link
4. Follow the link and create a new password
5. Log in with your new credentials

<Info>
  Password reset links expire after 1 hour for security reasons.
</Info>

## Account Security

### Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)

Add an extra layer of security to your account:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enable 2FA">
    Go to Profile → Security → Two-Factor Authentication and click "Enable".
  </Step>
  <Step title="Scan QR code">
    Use an authenticator app (Google Authenticator, Authy, etc.) to scan the QR code.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter verification code">
    Input the 6-digit code from your authenticator app to confirm setup.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save backup codes">
    Download and securely store your backup codes for account recovery.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Active Sessions

Monitor and manage your login sessions:

- View all devices currently logged into your account
- See login locations and timestamps
- Revoke access from specific devices
- Log out from all sessions at once

## Data Management

### Exporting Your Data

Download a copy of your account data:

1. Navigate to Profile → Privacy → Export Data
2. Select which data types to include
3. Click "Request Export"
4. Receive a download link via email within 24 hours

### Account Deletion

To permanently delete your account:

1. Go to Profile → Settings → Delete Account
2. Review the consequences of account deletion
3. Enter your password to confirm
4. Click "Delete Account" and confirm via email

<Note>
  Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data will be removed within 30 days.
</Note>

## Best Practices

<Card title="Profile Security Tips" icon="shield">
  - Use a strong, unique password
  - Enable two-factor authentication
  - Keep your email address up to date
  - Review active sessions regularly
  - Never share your password with anyone
  - Log out from shared or public devices
</Card>

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

**Can't update email**
- Verify the new email isn't already in use
- Check your spam folder for the verification email
- Ensure you're entering a valid email format

**Profile picture won't upload**
- Check file size (must be under 2MB)
- Ensure the file is in a supported format (JPG, PNG, WebP)
- Try a different browser if the issue persists

**Changes not saving**
- Check your internet connection
- Ensure all required fields are filled
- Try refreshing the page and submitting again

<Info>
  If you continue experiencing issues, contact support through the contact form with details about the problem.
</Info>
