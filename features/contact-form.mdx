The Tykode contact form provides an easy, secure way to reach out to the team for inquiries, project discussions, or support requests.

## Accessing the Contact Form

You can reach the contact form in several ways:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Via navigation menu">
    Click "Contact" in the main navigation header (visible on all pages).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Via call-to-action buttons">
    Click "Prendre contact" (Take Contact) buttons found throughout the site.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Direct URL">
    Navigate directly to `/contact` in your browser.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Contact Form Overview

The contact page is divided into two main sections:

**Left Side - Contact Information**
- Phone number with business hours
- Email addresses (contact and support)
- Physical address
- Social media links

**Right Side - Contact Form**
- Interactive message submission form
- Real-time validation
- Instant feedback on submission

## Filling Out the Form

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enter your information">
    Fill in the required fields marked with an asterisk (*):
    - Full name (minimum 2 characters)
    - Professional email (valid format required)
    - Subject (minimum 5 characters)
    - Message (minimum 20 characters)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add optional details">
    Optionally include:
    - Company name
    - Phone number
  </Step>
  <Step title="Accept privacy policy">
    Check the box to accept the privacy policy and data processing terms.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit your message">
    Click "Envoyer le message" (Send Message) to submit the form.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Form Fields

## Real-Time Validation

The contact form includes intelligent validation to ensure your message is properly formatted:

### Email Validation
- Checks for valid email format (name@domain.com)
- Shows error message if format is invalid
- Validates on blur (when you leave the field)

### Name Validation
- Ensures minimum 2 characters
- Shows remaining characters if too short
- Validates on blur

### Subject Validation
- Requires minimum 5 characters
- Shows how many more characters needed
- Validates on blur

### Message Validation
- Requires minimum 20 characters for meaningful communication
- Live character counter displays: "X/20 min"
- Counter turns from amber to gray when requirement met
- Validates on blur

<Info>
  Validation messages appear with a red alert icon and only show after you've interacted with each field.
</Info>

## Privacy and Security

### Privacy Policy Acceptance

Before submitting, you must:

1. Check the privacy policy acceptance checkbox
2. This confirms you understand how your data will be used
3. Required for GDPR compliance

### Anti-Spam Protection

The form includes built-in spam protection:

**Honeypot Field**
- Hidden "website" field invisible to human users
- Bots typically fill this field, flagging spam
- Legitimate submissions leave it empty

**Server-Side Validation**
- All data is validated on the server
- Prevents malicious submissions
- Rate limiting to prevent abuse

<Note>
  Your contact information is transmitted securely and used only to respond to your inquiry.
</Note>

## Submission Process

### When You Click Submit

<Steps>
  <Step title="Form validation">
    Client-side validation checks all required fields are complete and properly formatted.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sending state">
    The submit button shows a loading spinner with "Envoi en cours..." (Sending in progress).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Server processing">
    Your message is sent via tRPC to the backend for processing and storage.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirmation">
    You receive immediate feedback on success or error.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Success Confirmation

When your message is successfully sent:

1. **Visual Confirmation**
   - Green checkmark icon appears
   - Success message: "Message envoyé!" (Message sent!)
   - Thank you note confirming your message will be answered shortly

2. **Form Reset**
   - All fields are cleared
   - Ready for another message if needed
   - Option to "Send another message" appears

3. **Toast Notification**
   - Small notification appears at screen edge
   - Confirms successful submission
   - Automatically dismisses after a few seconds

### Error Handling

If submission fails:

1. **Error Message**
   - Red error notification appears
   - Describes what went wrong
   - Form data is preserved (not lost)

2. **Common Errors**
   - Missing required fields
   - Invalid email format
   - Network connection issues
   - Server timeout

3. **Resolution**
   - Check highlighted fields
   - Correct any validation errors
   - Try submitting again
   - Contact via phone or email if issue persists

<Info>
  If you continue experiencing submission errors, try refreshing the page or contact Tykode directly via email or phone.
</Info>

## Troubleshooting

### Form Not Submitting

**Check Required Fields**
- Ensure all fields marked with `*` are filled
- Verify email format is correct
- Check message meets 20 character minimum

**Privacy Policy**
- Confirm you've checked the privacy acceptance box
- This is required to submit

**Internet Connection**
- Verify your internet connection is stable
- Try refreshing the page

### Not Receiving Response

**Check Spam Folder**
- Responses may be filtered as spam
- Add contact@tykode.com to contacts

**Verify Email Address**
- Ensure you entered your email correctly
- Check for typos in your email address

**Wait Period**
- Allow 24-48 hours for initial response
- Business days only (Monday-Friday)

### Validation Errors

**Email Format**
- Must include @ symbol and domain
- Example: yourname@company.com

**Message Too Short**
- Must be at least 20 characters
- Counter shows characters remaining

**Name Too Short**
- Must be at least 2 characters
- Include first and last name

<Info>
  If you encounter persistent issues with the contact form, you can always reach Tykode directly via email at contact@tykode.com or by phone.
</Info>
