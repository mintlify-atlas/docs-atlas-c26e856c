## Welcome to Tykode

Tykode is your digital solutions partner in French Guiana, providing comprehensive services from website creation to mobile apps, e-commerce, and custom software development. This guide will walk you through everything you need to know to get started.

<Note>
  **New to Tykode?** This guide takes about 10 minutes to complete and will help you understand how to make the most of our platform.
</Note>

## Create Your Account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign up for a free account">
    Visit [tykode.com](https://tykode.com) and click the **Get Started** button in the top navigation.
    
    You'll need to provide:
    - Your full name
    - Professional email address
    - Secure password (minimum 8 characters)
    - Company name (optional)
    
    <Tip>
      Use your business email for easier collaboration with your team later.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Verify your email">
    Check your inbox for a verification email from Tykode. Click the verification link to activate your account.
    
    <Warning>
      The verification link expires after 24 hours. If it expires, you can request a new one from the login page.
    </Warning>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Complete your profile">
    After verification, you'll be prompted to complete your profile:
    - Add a profile picture (recommended)
    - Enter your phone number for faster communication
    - Specify your location in French Guiana
    - Describe your business or project briefly
    
    This information helps us provide better, more personalized service.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Make Your First Service Request

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose your service">
    Browse our [services page](/services/overview) and select the service that matches your needs. Click **Request This Service** or **Contact Us**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Fill out the service request form">
    Provide detailed information about your project:
    - **Project name** - What you want to build
    - **Business goals** - What you want to achieve
    - **Target audience** - Who will use your solution
    - **Timeline** - When you need it completed
    - **Budget range** - Your investment capacity
    
    <Tip>
      The more details you provide, the more accurate our proposal will be.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Submit and wait for response">
    After submitting your request:
    1. You'll receive an immediate confirmation email
    2. Our team reviews your request (usually within 24 hours)
    3. We'll contact you to schedule a discovery call
    4. You'll receive a detailed proposal with pricing and timeline
    
    <Info>
      **Response time**: We typically respond within 24 hours on business days (Monday-Friday, 9h-18h GMT-3).
    </Info>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Understanding the Service Process

Once you accept a proposal, here's what happens:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Discovery & Planning">
    We conduct an in-depth analysis of your needs:
    - Requirements gathering sessions
    - Technical feasibility assessment
    - Project scope definition
    - Detailed timeline creation
  </Step>

  <Step title="Design & Approval">
    You'll receive:
    - Visual mockups or wireframes
    - User experience flow diagrams
    - Branding elements (if applicable)
    
    You approve each design phase before we move forward.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Development">
    Our team builds your solution:
    - Regular progress updates (bi-weekly)
    - Demo sessions to show progress
    - Opportunity for feedback and adjustments
  </Step>

  <Step title="Testing & Launch">
    Final quality assurance:
    - Comprehensive testing across devices
    - User acceptance testing with your team
    - Training on how to use/manage your solution
    - Go-live deployment and monitoring
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Need Help?

<Accordion title="I can't verify my email">
  Check your spam folder first. If you still can't find it, click **Resend verification email** on the login page. If the problem persists, contact support@tykode.com.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How do I reset my password?">
  Click **Forgot password?** on the login page. Enter your email address and follow the instructions sent to your inbox. See [Managing Your Account](/guides/managing-account#password-management) for details.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What if I'm not sure which service I need?">
  No problem! Use our contact form to describe your project, and we'll recommend the best solution. You can also schedule a free consultation call.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I request multiple services at once?">
  Yes! Many clients combine services (e.g., website + e-commerce + digital marketing). Submit separate requests or mention multiple services in your initial inquiry.
</Accordion>
