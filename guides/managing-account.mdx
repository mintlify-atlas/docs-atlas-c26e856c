## Account Overview

Your Tykode account is your gateway to all our digital services. This guide covers everything you need to know about managing your account settings, updating your profile, and keeping your information secure.

<Tip>
  Keep your account information up to date to ensure smooth communication and service delivery.
</Tip>

## Accessing Your Account Settings

To access your account settings:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to your account">
    Visit [tykode.com](https://tykode.com) and sign in with your email and password.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Navigate to Settings">
    Click on your profile picture or name in the top-right corner, then select **Settings** from the dropdown menu.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose a settings section">
    Settings are organized into tabs:
    - **Profile** - Personal information
    - **Account** - Email, password, security
    - **Preferences** - Notifications, theme
    - **Billing** - Payment methods, invoices
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Profile Management

### Update Your Profile Information

Keep your profile current for better service:

<Accordion title="Personal Information">
  **What you can update:**
  - Full name
  - Profile picture
  - Phone number
  - Location/address
  - Company name
  - Job title/role
  
  **How to update:**
  1. Go to **Settings** > **Profile**
  2. Click **Edit** next to the field you want to change
  3. Make your changes
  4. Click **Save Changes**
  
  <Note>
    Changes are saved immediately and apply across all your active projects.
  </Note>
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Profile Picture">
  A professional profile picture helps our team recognize you:
  
  1. Click on your current picture or the placeholder icon
  2. Click **Upload New Photo**
  3. Select an image from your device (JPG, PNG, or GIF)
  4. Crop if desired
  5. Click **Save**
  
  **Requirements:**
  - Maximum file size: 5MB
  - Recommended dimensions: 400x400 pixels
  - Supported formats: JPG, PNG, GIF
  
  <Tip>
    Use a clear, professional headshot for the best results.
  </Tip>
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Business Information">
  If you're representing a business:
  
  **Add company details:**
  - Company name
  - Industry/sector
  - Company size
  - Website URL
  - Tax ID (SIRET) for invoicing
  
  This information appears on invoices and helps us understand your business context better.
</Accordion>

### Change Your Email Address

Your email is your primary identifier:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Request email change">
    1. Go to **Settings** > **Account**
    2. Click **Change Email**
    3. Enter your new email address
    4. Enter your current password for verification
  </Step>

  <Step title="Verify both emails">
    We'll send verification emails to:
    - Your **old email** - Click to confirm you requested this change
    - Your **new email** - Click to verify ownership
    
    <Warning>
      You must verify both emails within 24 hours, or the change request expires.
    </Warning>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Email updated">
    Once both verifications are complete, your email is updated. Use the new email for future logins.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  **Important**: All future communications, including project updates and invoices, will be sent to your new email address.
</Note>

## Password Management

### Change Your Password

Regularly updating your password enhances security:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access password settings">
    Go to **Settings** > **Account** > **Password**
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter passwords">
    - **Current password** - Your existing password
    - **New password** - Your desired password
    - **Confirm new password** - Re-enter for verification
    
    **Password requirements:**
    - Minimum 8 characters
    - At least one uppercase letter
    - At least one number
    - At least one special character (!@#$%^&*)
    
    <Tip>
      Use a password manager to generate and store strong passwords securely.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save and logout">
    Click **Update Password**. You'll be logged out automatically. Log back in with your new password.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Reset Forgotten Password

If you've forgotten your password:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Request reset">
    1. Go to the [login page](https://tykode.com/login)
    2. Click **Forgot password?**
    3. Enter your email address
    4. Click **Send Reset Link**
  </Step>

  <Step title="Check your email">
    You'll receive a password reset email within a few minutes. Click the **Reset Password** link.
    
    <Warning>
      Reset links expire after 1 hour. If expired, request a new one.
    </Warning>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create new password">
    1. Enter your new password (meeting requirements above)
    2. Confirm the password
    3. Click **Reset Password**
    4. You'll be redirected to log in with your new password
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Accordion title="Didn't receive the reset email?">
  Try these steps:
  1. **Check spam/junk folder** - Reset emails sometimes get filtered
  2. **Verify email address** - Ensure you entered the correct email
  3. **Wait a few minutes** - Email delivery can be delayed
  4. **Request again** - You can request multiple reset links
  5. **Contact support** - If still no email, reach out to support@tykode.com
</Accordion>

### Active Sessions

Monitor devices logged into your account:

1. Go to **Settings** > **Account** > **Active Sessions**
2. Review the list of active devices and locations
3. Click **End Session** next to any unrecognized device

<Warning>
  If you see suspicious activity, immediately change your password and contact support@tykode.com.
</Warning>

## Preferences & Customization

### Theme Settings

Customize your visual experience:

<Accordion title="Choose Your Theme">
  Tykode supports three theme options:
  
  **Light Mode**
  - Clean, bright interface
  - Best for daytime use
  - Reduces eye strain in bright environments
  
  **Dark Mode**
  - Dark background with light text
  - Easier on eyes in low-light conditions
  - Reduces blue light exposure
  
  **System Default**
  - Automatically matches your device theme
  - Switches between light/dark based on time of day
  
  **To change:**
  1. Go to **Settings** > **Preferences** > **Appearance**
  2. Select your preferred theme
  3. Changes apply immediately
  
  <Tip>
    Theme preference syncs across all your devices when you're logged in.
  </Tip>
</Accordion>

### Notification Preferences

Control how and when you receive updates:

<Accordion title="Email Notifications">
  Configure email notifications for:
  - **Project updates** - Milestones, progress reports
  - **Messages** - New messages from Tykode team
  - **Invoices** - Payment reminders and receipts
  - **Marketing** - Service updates and promotions (optional)
  
  **To customize:**
  1. Go to **Settings** > **Preferences** > **Notifications**
  2. Toggle each notification type on/off
  3. Set frequency (immediate, daily digest, weekly summary)
  4. Click **Save Preferences**
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="SMS Notifications">
  Opt in to SMS notifications for urgent updates:
  - Project launch confirmations
  - Critical issues requiring immediate attention
  - Appointment reminders
  
  **Requirements:**
  - Valid phone number in your profile
  - Enabled in **Settings** > **Preferences** > **SMS Alerts**
  
  <Note>
    SMS notifications are sent only for time-sensitive matters. Standard messaging rates may apply.
  </Note>
</Accordion>

### Language Preferences

Tykode supports French and English:

1. Go to **Settings** > **Preferences** > **Language**
2. Select your preferred language
3. Interface updates immediately

<Tip>
  Project communications will match your language preference, though we're primarily based in French Guiana and communicate in French by default.
</Tip>

## Billing & Payment Information

### Payment Methods

Manage your payment methods securely:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Add payment method">
    1. Go to **Settings** > **Billing** > **Payment Methods**
    2. Click **Add Payment Method**
    3. Choose your method (credit card, bank transfer details)
    4. Enter payment information
    5. Click **Save**
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set default method">
    Click **Set as Default** next to your preferred payment method for automatic billing.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Remove payment method">
    Click the three dots (⋯) next to a payment method and select **Remove**.
    
    <Warning>
      You cannot remove your only payment method if you have active subscriptions.
    </Warning>
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Invoices & Payment History

Access your billing history:

1. Go to **Settings** > **Billing** > **Invoices**
2. View all past invoices and payments
3. Click **Download PDF** to save a copy
4. Click **View Details** to see itemized charges

<Tip>
  Invoices are also emailed to you automatically when issued.
</Tip>

## Account Closure

### Temporarily Deactivate Your Account

Pause your account without losing data:

1. Go to **Settings** > **Account** > **Deactivate Account**
2. Select a reason (optional)
3. Click **Deactivate**

While deactivated:
- You cannot access your dashboard
- Active projects are paused (discuss with your project manager first)
- Data is preserved
- You can reactivate anytime by logging in

### Permanently Delete Your Account

<Warning>
  **This action is irreversible.** All your data, project history, and invoices will be permanently deleted.
</Warning>

To delete your account:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Review active projects">
    Ensure all projects are completed and invoices are paid. Contact your project manager to close any open items.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Request deletion">
    1. Go to **Settings** > **Account** > **Delete Account**
    2. Read the warning carefully
    3. Type your password to confirm
    4. Type "DELETE" in the confirmation box
    5. Click **Permanently Delete My Account**
  </Step>

  <Step title="Deletion processed">
    - You'll receive a confirmation email
    - Account deletion takes effect immediately
    - Data is permanently removed within 30 days per GDPR
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can I have multiple accounts?">
    Each person should have only one account. If you manage multiple businesses, you can add multiple company profiles within a single account. Contact support if you need assistance setting this up.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I add team members to my account?">
    Team collaboration features are available for enterprise clients. Contact sales@tykode.com to learn about team accounts.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What data does Tykode store about me?">
    We store:
    - Account information (name, email, phone)
    - Project communications and files
    - Billing and payment history
    - Usage analytics (anonymized)
    
    See our Privacy Policy on the Tykode website for complete details.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I export my data?">
    Yes! Go to **Settings** > **Account** > **Export Data** to download a copy of all your account information, project files, and communications.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I update my billing address?">
    Go to **Settings** > **Billing** > **Billing Information** and click **Edit Address**. This address appears on your invoices.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
