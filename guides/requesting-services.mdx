## How to Request a Service

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose your service">
    Browse the [services overview](/services/overview) and select the service that best matches your needs.
    
    <Tip>
      **Not sure which service?** Use the contact form to describe your project, and we'll recommend the best approach.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click 'Request This Service' or 'Contact Us'">
    You'll find request buttons:
    - At the top of each service page
    - At the bottom after reviewing details
    - In the main services overview page
  </Step>

  <Step title="Complete the service request form">
    Provide detailed information to help us understand your needs:
    
    **Required fields:**
    - Your name and email (auto-filled if logged in)
    - Company name
    - Service you're interested in
    - Project description (minimum 20 characters)
    
    **Optional but recommended:**
    - Phone number for faster communication
    - Preferred timeline
    - Budget range
    - Relevant files (mockups, examples, requirements)
    - Website URL (if existing)
    
    <Warning>
      The more details you provide, the more accurate and faster our proposal will be.
    </Warning>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Submit your request">
    Click **Send Request**. You'll receive:
    1. Immediate on-screen confirmation
    2. Email confirmation within minutes
    3. Your request is assigned to a specialist
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What Happens After You Submit

### Immediate Actions (Within 1 Hour)

<Steps>
  <Step title="Automated confirmation">
    You'll receive an email confirming we received your request, including:
    - Request ID for reference
    - Summary of what you requested
    - Expected response timeline
    - Emergency contact information
  </Step>

  <Step title="Request assignment">
    Your request is automatically assigned to the appropriate team specialist based on:
    - Service type
    - Project complexity
    - Specialist availability
    - Language preference
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Initial Review (Within 24 Hours)

<Steps>
  <Step title="Specialist reviews your request">
    Our team member:
    - Analyzes your project requirements
    - Identifies technical considerations
    - Prepares initial questions
    - Checks for similar past projects
  </Step>

  <Step title="First contact">
    You'll receive either:
    - **Email with follow-up questions** - If we need clarification
    - **Phone call** - For complex or urgent projects
    - **Meeting invitation** - To schedule a discovery call
    
    <Info>
      We aim to make first contact within 24 hours on business days (Monday-Friday, 9h-18h GMT-3).
    </Info>
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Discovery Phase (2-5 Days)

<Accordion title="Discovery Call or Meeting">
  A 30-60 minute conversation where we:
  - **Understand your business** - Your industry, target audience, competitors
  - **Define objectives** - What success looks like for this project
  - **Explore requirements** - Must-haves vs. nice-to-haves
  - **Discuss constraints** - Budget, timeline, technical limitations
  - **Answer your questions** - Anything you want to know about our process
  
  **Preparation tips:**
  - Have examples of what you like/dislike
  - Know your budget range
  - Understand your timeline constraints
  - List your main concerns or questions
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Proposal Development">
  After the discovery call, we create a detailed proposal:
  
  **Included in the proposal:**
  - **Project scope** - Exactly what we'll deliver
  - **Technical approach** - Technologies and methods we'll use
  - **Timeline** - Phase-by-phase schedule with milestones
  - **Pricing** - Detailed cost breakdown
  - **Terms** - Payment schedule, revisions, ownership
  - **Team** - Who will work on your project
  
  **Timeline:**
  - Simple projects: 1-2 days
  - Complex projects: 3-5 days
  
  <Tip>
    We send proposals via email as PDF documents. Review carefully and don't hesitate to ask questions.
  </Tip>
</Accordion>

### Proposal Review & Acceptance (Variable)

<Steps>
  <Step title="Review the proposal">
    Take your time to:
    - Read through all sections carefully
    - Compare with other options if considering alternatives
    - Discuss with stakeholders
    - Prepare questions or concerns
    
    <Note>
      Proposals are typically valid for 30 days. After that, pricing or availability may change.
    </Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Request clarifications or changes">
    If anything is unclear or doesn't match your expectations:
    - Email your questions to your assigned specialist
    - Request a follow-up call
    - Ask for proposal revisions
    
    **Common negotiation points:**
    - Scope adjustments (adding/removing features)
    - Timeline modifications
    - Payment schedule flexibility
    - Additional support or training
  </Step>

  <Step title="Accept the proposal">
    Once you're ready to proceed:
    1. Reply to the proposal email with acceptance
    2. Sign the digital contract (sent separately)
    3. Make the initial payment (usually 30-50% deposit)
    4. Project kickoff is scheduled
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Providing Effective Feedback

Help us deliver exactly what you want:

<Accordion title="Design Feedback">
  **Be specific about:**
  - What you like and why
  - What you don't like and why
  - Visual references for desired changes
  - Priority of each change request
  
  **Examples of good feedback:**
  - ✅ "The blue color (#2563EB) is too bright. Can we try a darker shade like #1E40AF?"
  - ✅ "The contact form should be more prominent. Maybe move it above the fold?"
  - ❌ "I don't like the colors" (too vague)
  - ❌ "Make it pop more" (unclear)
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Functional Feedback">
  **Test thoroughly and report:**
  - Steps to reproduce issues
  - Expected vs. actual behavior
  - Which device/browser you're using
  - Screenshots or screen recordings
  
  **Bug report template:**
  ```
  Issue: Button doesn't work on mobile
  Device: iPhone 13, iOS 16, Safari
  Steps to reproduce:
  1. Open contact page
  2. Fill out form
  3. Tap Submit button
  Expected: Form submits
  Actual: Nothing happens
  Screenshot: [attached]
  ```
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Content Feedback">
  **For text, images, and media:**
  - Proofread carefully
  - Check factual accuracy
  - Verify links work
  - Ensure images are appropriate
  - Confirm all legal text is correct
  
  <Warning>
    Content approval is your responsibility. We recommend having someone proofread before final approval.
  </Warning>
</Accordion>

### Revision Rounds

Most projects include revision rounds:

**Typical allowances:**
- **Design phase**: 2-3 revision rounds
- **Development phase**: 1-2 revision rounds
- **Final review**: 1 comprehensive revision

**What counts as a revision:**
- Significant design changes
- Scope modifications
- Complete page redesigns

**What doesn't count:**
- Bug fixes
- Minor tweaks (color adjustments, spacing)
- Content updates (text, images)
- Clarifications of existing work

<Tip>
  Consolidate feedback into single comprehensive reviews rather than requesting changes piecemeal. This speeds up the process.
</Tip>

## Testing & Launch

<Steps>
  <Step title="User Acceptance Testing (UAT)">
    Before launch, you thoroughly test everything:
    - All features work as expected
    - Content is accurate and complete
    - Design matches approved mockups
    - Mobile/tablet versions work properly
    - All browsers are supported
    
    **Testing checklist provided** with specific items to verify.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Training session">
    We provide training on:
    - How to use your new solution
    - Content management system (if applicable)
    - Basic troubleshooting
    - Where to get help
    
    **Format**: Video call with screen sharing + recorded for reference
  </Step>

  <Step title="Pre-launch preparation">
    Final steps before going live:
    - Domain setup/transfer (for websites)
    - SSL certificate installation
    - Analytics configuration
    - Backup systems verification
    - Performance optimization
  </Step>

  <Step title="Launch!">
    Your solution goes live:
    - We coordinate the launch timing with you
    - Monitor for the first 24-48 hours
    - Address any immediate issues
    - Provide post-launch report
    
    <Info>
      We typically recommend launching on Tuesday-Thursday to allow time for monitoring before the weekend.
    </Info>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Multiple Services & Bundles

### Combining Services

Many clients benefit from combining services:

**Popular combinations:**
- Website + Digital Marketing
- E-commerce + Maintenance Plan
- Visual Identity + Website
- Mobile App + Custom Backend
- Any service + Consulting

**Benefits:**
- **Discount**: 10-15% off when bundling 2+ services
- **Cohesive solution**: Everything works together seamlessly
- **Single point of contact**: One team managing everything
- **Faster delivery**: Parallel workstreams where possible

### Phased Approach

For large projects, consider a phased approach:

**Example: E-commerce Project**
1. **Phase 1** (Month 1-2): Visual identity and branding
2. **Phase 2** (Month 3-4): Basic e-commerce site (MVP)
3. **Phase 3** (Month 5-6): Mobile app
4. **Phase 4** (Ongoing): Marketing and optimization

**Advantages:**
- Spread costs over time
- Start generating revenue sooner
- Learn and adapt between phases
- Lower overall risk
