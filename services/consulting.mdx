# Technology Consulting & Strategy

Audit, strategy, technical roadmap. We help you make the right decisions before investing in development.

## Why Get a Consulting Audit?

<Note>
To avoid wasting money on unsuitable solutions. An audit helps you understand what you REALLY need, prioritize investments, and avoid costly mistakes. It's a time and money saver in the long run.
</Note>

## Our Expertise

### Custom Strategy
We analyze your market, competitors, and goals to define THE best digital approach for your business.

### Technical Audit
Analysis of your current infrastructure, identification of weaknesses, improvement recommendations, and optimization opportunities.

### Growth Roadmap
Concrete action plan with prioritized steps, quick wins to seize, and long-term vision to scale your business.

### Human Accompaniment
No incomprehensible technical jargon. We simplify everything so you understand the stakes and can decide knowledgeably.

### Optimized ROI
We identify investments that will have the most impact on your growth. No budget wasted on useless features.

### Technology Watch
We advise you on the latest technologies and trends relevant to your sector. Not too much, not too little.

## Our Process

### 1. Discovery & Scoping
First exchange to understand your business, goals, constraints, and ambitions. We ask the right questions.

**Duration: 1-2 days**

### 2. Audit & Analysis
In-depth analysis of your current situation: website, tools, processes, data. Benchmark with competitors.

**Duration: 3-5 days**

### 3. Strategy & Recommendations
We develop the digital strategy adapted to your context. Technology choices, action prioritization, budget forecast.

**Duration: 3-5 days**

### 4. Presentation & Action Plan
Presentation of strategy, roadmap, and budget. Q&A, adjustments if needed. You leave with a clear plan to move forward.

**Duration: 1 day**
