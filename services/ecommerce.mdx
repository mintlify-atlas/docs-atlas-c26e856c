## Click & Collect: Best of Both Worlds

Your customers order online and pick up in store. Zero delivery costs for them, foot traffic for you.

**How it works:**
1. Customers order online
2. They choose a pickup time slot
3. You prepare the order calmly
4. They pick up in store = zero shipping costs
5. Bonus: they often buy something else in-store

<Tip>
Click & Collect increases average cart value and brings customers to your physical location where they make additional purchases.
</Tip>
