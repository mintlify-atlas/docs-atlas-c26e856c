## How Tykode Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose your service">
    Browse our [services](/services/overview) and identify which solution best fits your needs. Not sure? Contact us and we'll help you choose the right approach.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Submit a request">
    Fill out our [contact form](/contact-form) with details about your project:
    - Type of service needed
    - Your budget range
    - Desired timeline
    - Project description
    
    The more details you provide, the more accurate our initial proposal will be.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Free consultation">
    Within 24 hours, you'll receive:
    - Detailed response to your inquiry
    - Initial recommendations
    - Invitation to schedule a free consultation call
    
    During the consultation, we'll discuss your goals, requirements, and answer all your questions.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Receive proposal">
    After the consultation, we'll send you a comprehensive proposal including:
    - Scope of work and deliverables
    - Project timeline with milestones
    - Transparent pricing breakdown
    - Terms and payment schedule
  </Step>

  <Step title="Project kickoff">
    Once you approve the proposal:
    - We schedule the project kickoff meeting
    - You'll meet your dedicated project team
    - We establish communication channels
    - Development begins with regular updates
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What to Prepare

Before contacting us, it's helpful to have:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Project goals and objectives">
    Think about what you want to achieve. Examples:
    - Increase online sales by 50%
    - Reduce manual work by automating invoicing
    - Launch a mobile app to improve customer loyalty
    - Establish brand identity for a new business
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Budget and timeline">
    Have a rough idea of:
    - How much you can invest (we work with various budgets)
    - When you need the project completed
    - Whether you prefer phased delivery or all-at-once
    
    Don't worry if you're uncertain - we'll help you define realistic expectations.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Target audience information">
    Who will use your solution?
    - Customer demographics (age, location, preferences)
    - B2B or B2C?
    - Current pain points your customers face
    - How you currently serve them
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Competitive landscape">
    If applicable, note:
    - Competitors you admire or want to differentiate from
    - What they do well and where they fall short
    - Websites, apps, or tools you find inspiring
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Content and assets">
    Gather any existing materials:
    - Logo and brand guidelines (if you have them)
    - Photos, videos, or graphics
    - Text content for your website
    - Product catalog or service descriptions
    
    Don't worry if you don't have these - we can help create them.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Do I need an account to request services?">
    No. Simply fill out our [contact form](/contact-form) with your project details. We'll respond within 24 hours to start the conversation.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What if I'm not sure which service I need?">
    That's completely normal! Contact us with a description of what you want to achieve. We'll recommend the best approach and may suggest combining multiple services for optimal results.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How quickly can you start my project?">
    Most projects can start within 1-2 weeks after proposal approval, depending on our current workload and your specific requirements. Urgent projects can sometimes be accommodated - just let us know your timeline.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I see examples of your work?">
    Yes! Contact us and we'll share relevant case studies and examples that match your industry and project type. We respect client confidentiality, so some examples may be anonymized.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do you offer payment plans?">
    Yes. For projects over €3,000, we offer phased payment tied to milestones:
    - Initial deposit (typically 30-40%)
    - Milestone payments during development
    - Final payment upon delivery
    
    Specific terms are outlined in each proposal.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What if I need changes after launch?">
    All projects include a warranty period (typically 30-90 days) for bug fixes. Additional features or changes can be added through:
    - Hourly support contracts
    - Monthly maintenance plans (from €99/month)
    - New project proposals for major enhancements
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
